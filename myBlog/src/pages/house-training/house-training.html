House Training Your Cat

1. Teach a cat to use a litter box or train them to use the bathroom. Most of my cats use the bathroom. They know how to do their business there. I taught them this by keeping them inside the bathroom for an hour after feeding them. The kittens however can learn this from their parent.

2. Provide the cat with a scratching post. Unless you want the door frames and sofas of your house with scratch marks, then skip this step. The scratch post will help the cat live a normal, healthy life since it is part of their nature which they got from their feline ancestors in the old age.
